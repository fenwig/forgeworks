About Data Sync
Blueprint data is sourced from the Star Citizen Wiki community API at api.star-citizen.wiki.
  • Armor, weapon, ammunition, and ship component blueprints are imported. Weapon attachments, liveries, and other non-craftable types are excluded.
  • Ingredient quantities are converted from SCU to cSCU (0.01 SCU) to match the app's internal format.
  • Armor category (Combat, Cosmonaut, Engineer, etc.) is not available from the API — set it manually in Blueprint Database after syncing.
  • After a sync, open Blueprint Database to review imported blueprints and mark items as owned.
  • Owned and tracked flags are preserved across re-syncs. Each blueprint is matched by its stable Wiki UUID, so a patch-day refresh will not lose your ownership data. Only blueprints removed entirely from the game will lose their flags.
⚠ Factory Reset
Clear all app data (material lots, orders, blueprints, crafting log, tracked flags). This cannot be undone. Use before a major refactor or data reset.
